Brand Story

Born from a love of the wild and a desire to simplify beauty, our brand set out to redefine fragrance—solid, natural, and portable. Tired of synthetic scents and plastic waste, we created a collection of genderless perfumes made entirely from plant-based ingredients, responsibly sourced and thoughtfully blended.

Every formula is handcrafted to honor nature's essence, while every detail—from biodegradable packaging to minimal design—reflects our deep respect for the planet. This is fragrance reimagined—for you, and for the earth.

Find nature anywhere

Why solid?

We create solid perfumes because we believe fragrance should care for both your skin and the planet. By removing water and unnecessary fillers, we craft concentrated scents using natural, skin-loving ingredients.

Our solid format eliminates the need for plastic spray mechanisms and reduces packaging waste—offering a cleaner, more sustainable way to wear fragrance every day.

We believe

Our commitment to the environment guides every step we take. From using biodegradable, recyclable packaging to sourcing natural, ethically harvested ingredients, we design every product to minimize impact and maximize care. We believe beauty should never come at nature's expense.
